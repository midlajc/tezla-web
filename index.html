What is TEZLA 2.2?

Tezla 2.2 is a tech fest conducted by the Department of Computer Science of WMO Arts and Science College, Muttil. In this fest there are wide variety of events having participants from various Departments.
Tezla is a platform for the students to develop and showcase technical proficiency as well as artistic talents. The curtain raise of the programme will be on 24th March 2022. Many inter-department competitions are to be held at the event

Events

EXPO

The Tech community of Dept. of CS hosts a Learning Hub and Gear Lab, Allowing students to be introduced with new and old technologies.

FIND ME OUT

Test your brain and crush the hidden words! Come and find win the prize.

CODING

Each participants are required to achieve a certain goal using code in C/C++ . The winner is selected according to effective logic of the program.

SPOT PHOTOGRAPHY

The Participants are required to submit the best photo taken according to the Subject given.

GAME FEST

The Main competition will have 3 knockout rounds, Consisting of 2 or 3 PC games. The fest will also host Android games as spot registrations.

KEYBOARD ASSEMBLING

Keyboard with missing keys are provided to participants and required to assemble the keyboard correctly. The winner is selected by time taken and correctness.

SPOT GAMES

Participants are challenged with fun involved instant games. Winner is selected considering overall performance.

SPOT DANCE

Participants are challenged with varieties of songs on spot. Winner is selected considering performance and instantaneity.
