What is TEZLA 2.2?

Tezla 2.2 is a tech fest conducted by the Department of Computer Science of WMO Arts and Science College, Muttil. In this fest there are wide variety of events having participants from all over the District.
Tezla is a platform for the students to develop and showcase technical proficiency as well as artistic talents. Tezla came into reality on 24th March 2022. Many inter-department and inter-college competitions were held at this event
